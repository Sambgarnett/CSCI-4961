CSCI-4961 Intro to Quantum Computing
Summer '24

Welcome to the RPI CSCI-4961 (Intro to Quantum Computing) home page! Here, you will find information on course content like lectures, lab content, homeworks, and class structure/logistics. Navigate the contents of this webpage using the sidebar to your left (hover your mouse on the left). Intro to Quantum Computing is one of the first quantum computing focused courses at RPI, aiming to broaden RPIs quantum computing community. This course teaches students basic quantum computing concepts like superposition, entanglement, quantum algorithms and quantum applications. This course takes a hands on approach utilizing RPI's on campus IBM Quantum System One for labs.
